6 to 6 — Logo Variations

V1 — Vertical Stack

Sun on top, moon on bottom, 6s on sides. Square format.

V2 — Compact

Horizontal layout with sun/moon stacked in center.

V3 — Integrated

Sun and moon become the circular parts of the 6s. Includes "to" text.

V4 — Minimal

Simple sun and moon with subtle glow. Clean and minimal.

V5 — Horizon

Sun and moon separated by horizon line. Emphasizes day/night divide.

V6 — Badge

Circular badge format. Good for favicons and app icons.
